Our Story

Founded by David Johnson, a former truck driver from Brooklyn who won the second-largest Powerball jackpot in New York Lottery history ($298.3 million), the David Johnson Foundation was established to give back to citizens in need.

After winning the lottery on December 26, 2018, David chose to use his fortune to help others facing the same financial struggles he once experienced as a working-class truck driver. Understanding firsthand the challenges of making ends meet, he created this foundation to provide direct financial assistance to citizens and families in crisis.

"I know what it's like to worry about paying rent, medical bills, and putting food on the table," says David Johnson. "This foundation is my way of paying it forward and ensuring that others don't have to face these challenges alone."

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our grant program

Our grants are designed for individual citizens and families in need of financial assistance. This includes single parents, seniors on fixed incomes, students facing financial hardship, individuals experiencing medical emergencies, and families dealing with housing instability or essential needs.

Our grants range from $10,000 to $30,000, depending on the specific need and circumstances. We assess each application individually to determine the appropriate amount that will make the most meaningful impact on your situation.

Grants can be used for essential needs including housing assistance, medical expenses, educational costs, emergency financial relief, basic living expenses, and other critical life necessities. We focus on helping citizens overcome financial barriers that prevent them from achieving stability and success.

We understand that financial needs can be urgent. Our review process typically takes 2-4 weeks from application submission. For emergency situations, we offer expedited review within 5-7 business days. You'll receive regular updates throughout the process.

No, our grants are not loans and do not need to be repaid. We believe in providing direct financial assistance to help citizens overcome challenges and build a better future for themselves and their families without the burden of additional debt.
