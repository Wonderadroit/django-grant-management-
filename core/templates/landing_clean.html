About Our Founder

David Johnson's journey from struggling truck driver to philanthropist began with a life-changing Powerball win in 2019. After paying off debts and securing his family's future, David remembered what it felt like to worry about rent, medical bills, and basic necessities.

Rather than simply enjoying his newfound wealth, David established this foundation to provide direct financial assistance to individuals and families facing similar hardships. Our grants support essential needs, education opportunities, healthcare costs, housing assistance, and emergency situations.

How We Help

Our grant program is designed to provide immediate financial relief and long-term support for individuals and families in need.

Essential Needs

Rent assistance, utility bills, groceries, and other basic necessities to help you get back on your feet.

Education & Training

Tuition assistance, vocational training, certification programs, and educational resources for career advancement.

Healthcare Support

Medical bills, prescription costs, dental care, and mental health services for you and your family.
